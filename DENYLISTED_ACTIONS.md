# Denylisted Actions

These actions are forbidden in this repository workflow unless the repository policy is formally changed by the human maintainer and the action remains legal, defensive, scoped, and controlled.

- Public target scanning.
- Exploit execution against third-party systems.
- Credential theft, capture, collection, display, or storage.
- Token/session abuse.
- Malware, persistence, evasion, stealth, or bypass guidance.
- Authentication or authorization bypass instructions.
- Exfiltration or covert collection.
- Destructive commands without explicit approval and rollback notes.
- Hiding, deleting, or falsifying audit evidence.
- Publishing secrets, private paths, hostnames, private IPs, account emails, internal IDs, screenshots with sensitive data, or raw logs.
- Claiming affiliation, endorsement, sponsorship, certification, or partnership without explicit evidence and approval.
